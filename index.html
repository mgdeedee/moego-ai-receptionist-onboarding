Describe your boarding accommodations, room types, pricing, what's included, and any room-specific rules. Include cat boarding if offered. Also note: what should owners bring for their pet's stay? What's NOT accepted (beds, toys, etc.)?
Daycare
Describe your daycare / group play services, packages, pricing, evaluation requirements, and any alternatives for dogs that don't qualify for group play.
Grooming
Describe your grooming services, pricing by pet size, à la carte options, specials, and any limitations. If grooming isn't offered at this location, leave blank.

Describe training programs, pricing, and how clients schedule. If training inquiries transfer to your team, leave blank and set routing in the Services section.
